Document Sizing Guide: A4, A5, & A6

Published June 17, 2026 by Fast Access Hub

At Fast Access, document printing is one of our most popular services. We have been printing, duplicating, and laminating files for Hounslow residents and business owners for 25 years. Whether you need an urgent travel ticket, a lease agreement, or promotional flyers, here is a helpful guide to choosing the correct document sizes and formatting them correctly.

A4, A5, or A6: Which Size is Right for You?

Choosing the correct paper dimension is essential for readability and professionalism:

  • A4 Sizing (210 x 297 mm): The standard size for most documents. Recommended for CVs, job applications, invoices, contracts, legal prints, and return labels.
  • A5 Sizing (148 x 210 mm): Half the size of A4. Perfect for menus, event brochures, checklists, and smaller notice flyers.
  • A6 Sizing (105 x 148 mm): Postcard dimensions. Great for pocket receipts, business announcement cards, and tiny leaflets.

What is Lamination and Why Do You Need It?

Lamination seals your document in a protective, clear plastic film. This is highly recommended for documents that will be handled frequently, exposed to moisture, or stored long-term (such as signage, instructional notices, and certificates).
